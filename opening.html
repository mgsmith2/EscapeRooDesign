Description

Let’s talk about Vincent Van Gogh. This Duch post-impressionist painter became one of the most beloved artists all around the world after his death in 1890.
Van Gogh paved the way for modern artists with his unique brushstrokes and color pallet. He was one of the first artists who took their childhood
trauma and turned it into something beautiful. All of his paintings told a little big of his story. His official cause of death was ruled
out to be suicide, but there are many skeptics out there that believe there’s no way that he would have done something like that.

It’s up to you to try and figure it out. We will be bringing you back to his old home to look for more clues on what his cause of death really was.

Audio Recording


In this audio recording, you will have to be listening very closely to pick up on the three numbers that are mentioned.
Also, listen to what color the safe is that we are looking for. there will be a popup password that you put the color into to get to the next puzzle.

Open safe with the code

To open the safe you are going to be figuring out what order the three numbers go in. a popup password will be used once you figure out the
right order. once opened you’ll be led to the next puzzle.

Find the missing pease of the painting

This puzzle will have a ripped painting in the middle and five pieces of ripped canvas that could be a match to the real painting.
Each piece will have a three-letter code on it. That three-letter code will be used in the popup password to get you to the next puzzle.

Find hiden message in the fixed Canvas

You have a full painting in front of you now. But look very closely there is a hidden message that you’ll need to find. Once you find the message you can move on.

Message opens box

The message you find will lead you to the box with a special key in it. Examine all of the boxes and try to find th right one.

Find the door that the key opens

Once the key is found you must pick which door the key will fit into. Once you find the right one you are on your way to the next escape room.

Conclusion

The conclusion will be a print-out poster of one of Van Gogh’s paintings of your choice.
